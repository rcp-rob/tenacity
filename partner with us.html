Quality Management System

Learning process, selection of duly qualified facilitators, assessors and moderators are all underpinned by principles of quality as detailed in the company's Quality Management System.

Unlimited Learner Support System

Unrestricted channels of communications are fundamental in providing continuous guidance before, during and after completion of training.
Our learner support includes face to face consultation, telephonic and web based support. Learner support also ensures continuous mentorship, coaching and support to the delegates keeping in mind the expected relevant application of what is learnt to the respective work environment.

Skills / Learning Programmes

All learning programs are well planned, consistent, incorporated, add value and prepare candidates for new career paths and positive promotional prospects within their respective work environment.
The outcomes of all learning programmes provide much needed capacity building where there are skills shortages and are geared towards enhancing economic participation, self-empowerment and employment.
